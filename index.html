Introduction

Dubai is one of the most luxurious places one earth from theme parks to restaurants to the tallest building in the world! In this travel website we will show you some of the most exotic places to go to in the amazing city of Dubai!

Location

Dubai is located in The United Arab Emirates, also known as The UAE. It is in the middle east of Asia. It the borders the countries Abu Dhabi to the south, Sharjah to the northeastand Oman to the southeast.

Population

The population of Dubai peaked at 3.331 million in 2019. Most of the city's population are tourist from other countries and cities from all around the world!
